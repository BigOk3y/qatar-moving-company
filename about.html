Our Story

Qatar's Moving Company, Built by People Who Moved Here Too

Qatar Moving Services was founded in 2012 by a small team who understood first-hand how stressful relocation can be in a fast-growing city. Having moved their own families and careers to Qatar, they knew the difference a reliable, professional moving company could make — and the gap that existed in the market.

What started as a two-truck operation in Doha has grown into a full-service relocation company trusted by over 1,200 families and businesses across the country. But our core philosophy hasn't changed: treat every move like it's our own.

Today, Qatar Moving Services is part of BigOak Corp — and we continue to uphold the same standards of transparency, professionalism, and care that have earned us our reputation.

Our Values

Honesty First

We quote what we charge. No inflated estimates, no surprise add-ons on moving day. The price we give you is the price you pay.

Your Belongings Are Safe

Every move is covered by our transit insurance policy. We use professional-grade materials and trained handlers for everything — from everyday furniture to one-of-a-kind antiques.

Respect for Your Time

We show up when we say we will. Our teams are punctual, briefed, and ready to work the moment they arrive — because your moving day is already a big day.

Local Expertise

We know Qatar — building access rules, permit requirements, peak traffic times, and service elevator protocols. That knowledge saves hours on moving day.

Clear Communication
